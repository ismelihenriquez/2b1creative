Saiyan Fitness

iOS & Android App Development

The Challenge

The client needed a simple, robust mobile app for users to access workout programs, track macros, and monitor their fitness progress on both iOS and Android.

The Solution

We designed and developed a cross-platform application with an intuitive interface. The app allows users to easily manage their fitness journey, from workout selection to detailed progress tracking.

The Results

  • Increased User Engagement: A streamlined UI led to higher daily active users.
  • Seamless Tracking: Simplified macro and progress tracking for better user retention.
  • Cross-Platform Launch: Successfully deployed on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

ROasted Coffee & Workspaces

Mobile App Development & Automation

The Challenge

ROasted was missing out on workspace bookings and revenue because their baristas, busy with coffee orders, couldn't handle phone reservations efficiently.

The Solution

We developed a seamless mobile app allowing customers to order coffee, book workspaces, manage rewards, and even unlock room doors directly from their phone. This fully automated the booking and check-in process, freeing up staff entirely.

Construction PM CRM

Custom Software Development

  • Enhanced Client Communication: Real-time progress sharing.
